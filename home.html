Let's Chat

Let's Chat is an attempt to deconstruct the standard chatting room. The website provides an immersive communication experience for the viewers. Music, motion graphics, and interaction are the key features of the website. The background is a motion graphic, the music search bar is on the right, and the sending text bar is on the bottom, allowing people to have Danmaku. Danmaku (literally "bullet curtain" or "curtain fire") is a Japanese term for an online video platform's subtitle system that allows users to write moving comments on a film as it plays.
